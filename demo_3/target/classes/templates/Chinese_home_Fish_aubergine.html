Fish aubergine

According to legend, a long time ago there was a businessman in Sichuan. The people in their family liked to eat fish. They also pay attention to the seasoning, so they are burning fish. Put some onions, ginger, garlic, wine, vinegar, soy sauce, etc. to add flavor to the seasoning. One night, when the hostess in the house was cooking another dish, she didn’t let the ingredients waste. She put the leftover ingredients in the last time the fish was fried in the dish. At the time, she thought This dish may not taste very good. Maybe the man in the house is not good after returning. When she is in a daze, her husband is going home to do business.

This husband doesn't know if he is hungry or feels the speciality of this dish. He hasn't waited for the meal to grab his hand and swallow it in his mouth. He hasn't waited for a minute. He can't wait to ask his wife what the dish is. At the end of the stutter, she accidentally discovered that her husband repeatedly praised the deliciousness of her dish. Her husband saw that she did not answer, and asked, "This is so delicious.

What did you do? The wife told him about it once and for all.

This dish is made with fried fish ingredients and other dishes, will be memorable, so named after the fish fry, hence the name.

Later, this dish has been improved by Sichuanese for several years and has been included in Sichuan recipes early, such as fish-flavored pork liver, fish-flavored pork, fish-flavored eggplant and fish-flavored silk. Nowadays, the unique flavor of the dish is very popular among people from all over the world, and it is popular in China.

Baking method
Preheat the baking tray for 5 minutes.
Drain the oil and fish with the shovel, pour the seasoning, carefully put into the eggplant, cover, and heat for 2 minutes.
Draw the eggplant to the circumference of the plate, leave the space in the middle, pour the powdered water, stir and heat for 1 minute.
Mix the eggplant and juice, pour in the sesame oil, and sprinkle it into the pan.
