Sirloin steak

According to one tradition, after tasting the delicacy of steak, Henry viii sealed it with Sir, or Sir+loin, which translates as sirloin in Chinese.

Sirloin steak (Sir Loin), is mainly composed of the spine of the upper waist, sirloin steak according to the different quality can be divided into small sirloin steak (entrecte) and sirloin steak (sirloin steak). Sirloin is actually a combination of the French words Sur (above) and Loin (above fillet), the meat above the fillet. Each one is about 250-300 grams. Sir Loin is lower loin, also known as the New Yorker, because the lower loin exercises more than filicarondo, so this part of the meat is thicker.

The meat is thin, juicy and tender. Frying steaks also has a lot of attention, both to be able to heat the surface of the beef quickly, but not to make their fiber rupture, this requires the experience and skills of the chef. A Fried sirloin steak will send out a filar silk fragrance, acetic acid juice poured around the steak, steak presents a very unique dark gold, eat knife cut steak, the beef inside is beautiful pink, juice is a lot of. Pick up a small piece of steak into the mouth, can clearly feel the juice as the teeth chew and spread out overflow, barbecue flavor is very good to transfer to the mouth of every taste bud, in which the circle, the original taste of beef play incisively and vividly.
